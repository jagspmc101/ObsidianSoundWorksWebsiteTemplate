About Obsidian SoundWorks


My Name is Jacob, I've been passionate about music for as long as I can remember. I started my musical journey on guitar, but my love for music quickly led me to explore DAWs, producing and recording. Over the years, I've worked hard to have the skills to create music for a wide range of projects, including video games, videos, podcasts, intros, and anything else that needs a unique sound.

I'm proud to say that I can work in almost any genre. I thrive on experimenting and pushing the boundaries of what’s possible, always seeking to create something fresh and exciting. My background in jazz also plays a key role in my musical approach, as I was fortunate enough to be part of the jazz bands at both my middle school and high school. Those experiences shaped my love for both structure and improvisation in music.
My Goal: Building Obsidian Soundworks
One of my biggest goals right now is to build and grow my business, Obsidian Soundworks. This is the name I use to sell my music, and it's become an exciting journey in building a brand that represents the diverse, experimental, and creative side of my music. I’m working hard to develop a presence in the music industry and offer high-quality compositions for a variety of media. As I continue to expand Obsidian Soundworks, I look forward to collaborating with even more creatives, pushing the boundaries of sound, and offering music that adds value to any project.Home
